New Metro Extension Opens in Ghaziabad

Oct 25th, 2025

Ghaziabad residents celebrated today as the new DMRC metro extension was officially opened. This project is expected to reduce traffic congestion and make daily travel easier for thousands of people.

"This metro extension is a big step towards better connectivity and development in Ghaziabad," said a government official.

Main Benefits of the Metro Extension:

  • Faster travel between Ghaziabad and Delhi
  • Eco-friendly public transport option
  • Improved job opportunities in nearby areas
