City Launches Tree Plantation Drive

Author: Apoorva Jaiswal | 28 September 2025

The city has started a new tree plantation campaign to improve air quality and provide more green spaces for citizens. Over 10,000 saplings will be planted in the first phase of the program.

According to officials, the initiative will not only help reduce air pollution but also promote biodiversity in the region. Local schools and colleges have been invited to take part in the drive.

Key Highlights:

  • 10,000 saplings to be planted this year
  • Involvement of schools and colleges
  • Focus on urban parks and roadside plantations
"This campaign is a step towards a cleaner and greener city," said the Mayor.
